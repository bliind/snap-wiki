[Abomination](<https://snap.fan/cards/Abomination/>)
- Gains a [new ability](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221881657884414042>) when paired with [High Evolutionary](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221881368401678397>).
[Absorbing Man](<https://snap.fan/cards/AbsorbingMan/>)
- Copies the entire text of the last card you played if it has an on reveal ability, meaning he will copy [Electro](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221880081451057213>) and [Space Stone](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221893053590016000>)'s on reveal abilities AND Ongoing abilities.
- Cards pulled by [Jubilee](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221883886682767512>), Sakaar, Grand Central, etc. are not played, Absorbing man will copy the card played before those effects triggered.
- Once Absorbing Man gets a different effect, his old one is gone. Meaning that if you play [Odin](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221888369408344224>) or another Absorbing Man, the new effect will be re-triggered/copied.
- Even after copying a different effect, Absorbing Man is still Absorbing Man. copying [Thanos](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221892858198364251>) with him won't give him the Power Stone +10 buff, copying [Thor](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221893413507436574>) won't give him the Mjolnir +6 buff.
[Adam Warlock](<https://snap.fan/cards/AdamWarlock/>)
- Adam Warlock's effect triggers at the end of the turn. End of the turn effects trigger in play order, meaning:
  if I play [Sunspot](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221892548017258517>) on turn one, Adam Warlock on turn 5 and the opponent plays [High Evo Cyclops](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221881528255254578>) on turn 6, they will trigger in this order: Sunspot gains power, Adam Warlock draws a card if im winning the lane and Cyclops afflicts two of my cards in that location with -1 power.
- If Adam Warlock is revealed at the end of the game (via Dark Dimension or [Supergiant](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221892700958359592>) for example), he won't draw a card, even if you're winning the lane
- End of the turn card effects (like this one) trigger after end of the turn location effects (like Asgard or Warrior Falls)
[Aero](<https://snap.fan/cards/Aero/>)
- Cards pulled by Sakaar, Grand Central, [Jubilee](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221883886682767512>), etc. do not count as played, Aero won't move them.
[Agatha Harkness](<https://snap.fan/cards/AgathaHarkness/>)
- At the start of the game, Agatha adds herself to your hand as a bonus draw and not the first draw like [Quicksilver](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221889150731681842>).
- Despite what her text says, Agatha starts in your deck (and is added to hand at the start of the game as mentioned above), meaning she can be copied by [Loki](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221884951725871176>) and [Quinjet](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221889306365263883>) won't reduce her cost.
- When Agatha is active in your hand, your turn timer is greatly reduced.
- Agatha plays your cards completely randomly, but she will play herself if she can (to a random location).
- When Ego is one of the locations, he'll take over and play your cards for you even if Agatha is in your hand.
- At the end of the game, Agatha will receive all boosters gained that match.
- Even turns means 2, 4 and 6.

[Angela](<https://snap.fan/cards/Angela/>)
- Angela's effect triggers when you stage a card to her locations as long as it reveals in that location. if you play a card there and it was moved while unrevealed (by [Juggernaut](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221883936607699045>) for example), Angela won't get the buff.

- Angela's effect wont be triggered by cards that were added/moved to her location (ex: [Doctor Doom](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221879809517289563>)'s Doombots and [Nightcrawler](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221888202172928020>)'s movement).
[Annihilus](<https://snap.fan/cards/Annihilus/>)
- When Annihilus' ability triggers, he checks cards' current power. This means if you have an [Iron Man](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221883462995284119>) on Sewer system, Annihilus will send him to the opponents side because his current power is -1. 
- Annihilus switches cards based on reveal order. if I have a lane with: [The Hood](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221893260482711656>) and [Sentry](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221890594687942687>)'s Void, The Hood will switch sides first, then The Void will switch.
- Annihilus **can** send himself to the opponents side if his power is lower than 0.

[Apocalypse](<https://snap.fan/cards/Apocalypse/>)

- "When you discard this" effects (ex: [Helicarrier](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221881233710256288>) and [Proxima Midnight](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221889052324663477>)) go before "When you discard a card effects (ex: [Miek](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221886391550607420>) and [Black Knight](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221877346647277609>)).
[Armor](<https://snap.fan/cards/Armor/>)
- Armor's effect protects both revealed and unrevealed cards at her location.
- [Galactus](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221880369079521400>) will destroy any card Armor is protecting, including Armor herself.
- When Armor moves, her effect turns on immediately; not when she resolves.
[Arnim Zola](<https://snap.fan/cards/ArnimZola/>)
- Arnim Zola must destroy a card in order to create copies. If a card is indestructible, he won't be able to copy it.
- Arnim Zola creates two copies, the left copy's ability will activate before the right one's.
- The rightmost copy acts like an unrevealed card (even if its visually revealed), until the leftmost copy resolves.
[Attuma](<https://snap.fan/cards/Attuma/>)
- Attuma looks at both his own and his targets' __**current**__ power.
- Attuma doesn't necessarily destroy the card with the *lowest* power in his lane; he can destroy any card on your side that has less power than him.
- Attuma has an end of the turn effect. End of the turn effects trigger based on play order.
  example: if I play [Sunspot](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221892548017258517>) on turn 1, Attuma on turn 4 (with priority) and the opponent plays [High Evo Cyclops](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221881528255254578>) on turn 4 (without priority), the effects will trigger in this order:
  Sunspot gains power, Attuma will destroy himself if nothing is stopping him from doing so and Cyclops afflicts two of my cards in that location with -1 power.
- Attuma's effect can be "cancelled" if you make him reveal at the end of the game using Dark Dimension, [Invisible Woman](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221883056282009620>) or [Supergiant](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221892700958359592>).
[Baron Mordo](<https://snap.fan/cards/BaronMordo/>)
- If your opponent has a [Mobius](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221886921366700185>) in play, he'll ignore Mordo's cost increase; Baron Mordo won't be able to set the card's cost to 6 (unless that card costs more than 6) as long as Mobius' ability is active.
- A friendly Mobius won't stop the affected card's cost from going back down at the end of turn 6.
- Baron Mordo's cost increase turns off at the start of turn 6, not the end of it.
- The affected card's cost can't be reduced before turn 6, when Mordo's effect turns off.
 - [Wave](<https://discord.com/channels/978545345715908668/1221858388401717279/1221894381418709103>) overrides Mordo's effect and reduces the card's cost to 4 even before turn 6.
